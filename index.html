What Exactly Is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin (BTC) is the world's premier secure decentralized cryptocurrency. It acts as an open global monetary framework that functions completely separate from central banks, commercial oversight boards, or federal interference.

By operating on a distributed cryptographic record system called the Blockchain, Bitcoin balances are guaranteed mathematically. With a hardcoded maximum cap of exactly 21 Million units ever to exist, it cannot be duplicated, inflated, or diluted by central financial structures.
